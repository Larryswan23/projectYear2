Coast Guard

The Irish Coast Guard (IRCG; Irish: Garda Cósta na hÉireann is part of the Department of Transport of Ireland.

The primary roles of the Coast Guard include maritime safety and search and rescue. The Irish Marine Search and Rescue Region (IMSRR)[2] is the area over which the Coast Guard has responsibility. [3] This area is bordered by the UK Search and Rescue Region.
